Welcome to E-health4u

What is Ehealth4u?

Ehealth4u is a django app that allows you to register create patient with their medical records attached .

How do I start?

First, create a new patient by filling out the form on this page. Once an patient is created you will be able to add medical records.

What are some Patient Image best practices?

  • Have a resolution of 512x512
  • Use common image formats such as .JPG, .GIF, or .PNG
  • Remain under the 2MB limit.
  • Square images look best
